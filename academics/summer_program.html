Summer Camp 2016

Summer Program

This summer open your child's eyes to the wonder of nature around them. The American Montessori School, opposite the Duke Farms in Hillsborough, has a fun-filled, educative nature camp this summer. Each program is instructed by a trained teacher in a Montessori environment. Camp themes include Flower Power, Under Sea Adventure, Water Wizard, World of Bubbles, Scales and Tails, Bug’s World and Gardening.

In addition to the nature camp, full day campers will participate in group activities such as: cooking, water play, storytelling, sewing, crafts, art, board games, woodworking, ball games, jewelry making, creative writing, Legos, etc. Tours of the site may be made during the school year by calling (908) 977-6640. See below for the summer camp fees and schedule.
