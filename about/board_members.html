Meet our Board of Advisors

  • Ms.Maria is a veteran in the Montessori field, with over 30 years of experience teaching, managing and running Montessori schools in the central Jersey area. She has immense experience in Montessori curriculum development and has presented courses on Montessori Philosophy to teachers and parents. She has served as mentor and guide to new Montessori teacher trainees as part of her role at the Center for Montessori Teacher Education. She is passionate about the Montessori field of education and spreading its reach to every child.
