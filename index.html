Welcome to American Montessori School in Hillsborough!

"Our care of the child should be governed, not by the desire to make him learn things, but by the endeavor always to keep burning within him that light which is called intelligence."

—Maria Montessori

The greatest investment you can make for your child is to provide her with a strong educational foundation. At American Montessori School, students are given an academic experience that exceeds most traditional settings.

The American Montessori School, conveniently located near the intersection of U.S. 206 and Dukes Parkway W, is a private, non-sectarian Montessori school for children aged 2.5 to 6 years old. The school features fully-equipped classrooms, well-maintained playgrounds and is staffed by trained Montessori teachers.

The American Montessori School is licensed by the New Jersey Department of Youth and Family Services and is affiliated with The American Montessori Society, a professional organization that endorses authentic Montessori schools all over the world.

Our students come from a wide range of towns and cultural backgrounds. Paired with the multi-national American Montessori staff, they create a rich and diverse cultural canvas.

At AMS, we believe in developing the whole-child intellectually, socially, emotionally and physically. We are committed to delivering an outstanding experience for your child based on the proven Montessori methodology and the great talents and dedication of our faculty.

Our admissions process is designed to help you make that very important decision of choosing a school for your child and your family. You can then make an informed choice whether our school meets your expectations for the education of your child.
